Reading Telugu Diacritics

Don't know how to read the strange-looking diacritics (ḍ, ḷ, ś, etc.) on this site? This short guide will teach you.

Telugu (and many other Indic languages) contain sounds not found in English. Since this site does not use the Telugu script, we have to use diacritics to write the language. There are many schemes for writing Telugu with English letters; this site uses the IAST Transliteration, one of the most popular schemes used for scholarly works.
